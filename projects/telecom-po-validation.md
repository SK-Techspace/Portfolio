# Telecom PO Validation and Revenue Protection

## Business Problem

Telecom purchase order and quotation validation relied heavily on manual checks, creating risk around missed discrepancies, delayed reviews, and revenue leakage. The process also consumed analyst time that could have been used for higher-value operational support.

## Stakeholders

- Finance
- Sales
- Program management
- Operations teams

## Tools Used

- Alteryx
- SQL
- Excel
- Power BI

## Approach

I mapped the validation workflow end to end, identified where data mismatches were occurring, and translated those issues into automated ETL logic. I built more than 10 Alteryx workflows to validate telecom PO and quotation data, introduced automated quality checks, and supported downstream reporting so teams could review exceptions faster.

Alongside the technical implementation, I worked with finance, sales, and program stakeholders to align business rules, clarify exception criteria, and make sure the output supported day-to-day operational decisions.

## Outcome

- Prevented **$200K+ revenue leakage**
- Reduced **manual review effort by 40%**
- Improved consistency and speed of operational validation
- Strengthened trust in revenue-related telecom data

## Portfolio Relevance

This project demonstrates a blend of data automation, quality control, process redesign, and stakeholder coordination, which is highly relevant for **Data Analyst**, **Business Analyst**, **Project Analyst**, and **BI Analyst** roles.
