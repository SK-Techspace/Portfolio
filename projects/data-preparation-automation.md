# Data Preparation Automation for Cross-Functional Reporting

## Business Problem

Recurring reporting processes were slowed by manual ingestion, cleansing, and preparation steps across multiple teams. The lack of a streamlined workflow increased turnaround time and made it harder to maintain consistency in reporting outputs.

## Stakeholders

- Finance
- Sales
- Program teams
- Reporting consumers across cross-functional teams

## Tools Used

- Alteryx
- Excel
- SQL
- Power BI

## Approach

I developed RPA-style automation pipelines to streamline raw data ingestion and preparation for recurring reporting needs. The solution focused on reducing repetitive manual tasks, improving consistency in data preparation, and making downstream dashboards and reporting faster to maintain.

Because the workflow supported multiple teams, I coordinated with users to understand reporting pain points, define input and output requirements, and ensure the automation fit into the broader reporting and delivery process.

## Outcome

- Reduced **data preparation time by 50%**
- Improved reporting consistency across **4 cross-functional teams**
- Lowered manual effort in recurring reporting operations
- Created a more scalable workflow for future reporting expansion
