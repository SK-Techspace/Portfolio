# Procurement Analytics and Supplier Performance Insights

## Business Problem

Procurement and supply-related teams needed better visibility into sales trends, supplier performance, and sourcing patterns to reduce mismatches between demand and supply while improving cost efficiency.

## Stakeholders

- Procurement
- Supply chain teams
- Sales operations
- Business leadership

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- SQL
- SAP HANA
- Excel

## Approach

I built ETL pipelines to extract supplier and procurement data from SAP HANA and transformed it into analysis-ready datasets. I then created Power BI dashboards to track sales, supply chain KPIs, procurement trends, and supplier performance.

The work combined technical delivery with business coordination by aligning the dashboard outputs to procurement decision-making needs and focusing the analysis on actionable trends rather than static reporting.

## Outcome

- Reduced **demand-supply mismatches by 20%**
- Identified **15% cost-saving opportunities**
- Improved visibility into procurement trends and supplier performance
- Supported more data-driven sourcing decisions
