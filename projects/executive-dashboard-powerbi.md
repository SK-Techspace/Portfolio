# Executive Dashboard for Program and Revenue Tracking

## Business Problem

Leadership needed a clearer view of project milestones, revenue movement, and compliance metrics across ongoing infrastructure-related programs. Existing reporting was fragmented and required manual consolidation from multiple sources.

## Stakeholders

- Leadership and executives
- Program management
- Finance
- Operations

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Excel
- SQL

## Approach

I designed a reporting framework that translated operational and financial data into leadership-ready dashboards. The build focused on milestone visibility, revenue flow, and compliance tracking, using clear KPIs, visual summaries, and interactive components that supported quick review and drill-down.

From a project execution perspective, I gathered requirements from business teams, aligned metric definitions, and iterated on the dashboard design based on stakeholder feedback to ensure the reporting stayed usable and decision-oriented.

## Outcome

- Improved executive visibility into milestones and revenue performance
- Reduced time spent manually consolidating reporting inputs
- Enabled faster stakeholder review cycles and more consistent status tracking
